Case Studies

Read how XTT designs, rolls out, and operates secure software-defined networks that recover productivity loss, eliminate single points of ISP failure, and support enterprise edge compliance.

1. Multi-Site Logistics Firm: Managed SD-WAN

The Challenge: A regional distribution logistics company with 52 warehouse locations was experiencing frequent network outages due to local broadband failures, resulting in scanner gun disconnects and shipping delays costing $12k per hour.

The Solution: XTT deployed a carrier-neutral Managed SD-WAN solution at each warehouse. We bonded local primary fiber links with dynamic cellular LTE/5G backups routing directly through our private Atlanta core node.

2. WFH Engineering Team: CGNAT Bypass & Security

The Challenge: A software firm had 120 developers working remotely. Their local residential ISPs placed endpoints behind Carrier-Grade NAT (CGNAT), making secure port-forwarding and direct SSH configurations to localized staging servers impossible.

The Solution: Deployed XTT Residential SD-WAN client tunnels. Tunnels bypassed local CGNAT bounds, routing connections directly to our core transit node to inject a clean, static, dedicated IPv4 address for each developer with strict firewall profiling.

3. Rural Construction Site: Bonded Transit

The Challenge: A mining and heavy construction company stood up a large field base camp in a rural region with zero wireline broadband access, needing low-latency VoIP and heavy document sync links.

The Solution: Installed an XTT Rural Connectivity Kit containing a high-gain MikroTik Chateau gateway bonding a Starlink Dishy with a secondary long-range LTE directional receiver, tunnelled directly to our nearest regional transit node.
