Project Management & Integration

Deploy, migrate, and orchestrate network rollouts with zero business disruption.

The Risk of Chaotic Network Deployments

Network migrations are high-risk operations. When organizations deploy SD-WAN or transition to SASE without structured project management, they face configuration errors, inconsistent security policies, missed carrier dispatch windows, and unexpected downtime. Coordinating local ISPs, hardware staging, field technicians, and internal security approvals often overwhelms internal IT divisions.

XTT's Integration Lifecycle Framework

XTT delivers network engineering projects using a structured, four-phase delivery framework that guarantees clean handovers. We take end-to-end ownership of the process—handling audit, device staging, field dispatch coordination, and post-installation validation so your team doesn't have to manage the details.

Key Integration Benefits

Pre-Configured Staging

All CPE routers (FortiGate or MikroTik) are staged and tested at XTT Atlanta configuration labs before shipping, eliminating out-of-box failures.

Zero-Downtime Migration

Structured cutover schedules managed by certified PMPs ensure services migrate dynamically during minimal-impact windows.

Carrier Coordination

XTT handles communication with local exchange carriers and ISPs, resolving provisioning roadblocks directly.

Detailed Handover

Receive complete network diagrams, configuration sheets, IP schedules, and diagnostic logs for your NOC team.
