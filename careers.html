Why Work at XTT?

While legacy telecom conglomerates rely on outdated hardware lock-ins and manual customer service models, XTT is built on automation, engineering integrity, and carrier-neutral flexibility. We empower our teams to design elegant, high-availability SD-WAN overlays, deploy unified SASE policies on our core BGP anycast exchanges, and consult on the next generation of local edge AI architectures. If you thrive on solving complex routing issues and building clean network paths, you will fit right in.

Our Cultural Pillars

Continuous Learning

Technology evolves rapidly. We support our team's growth with dedicated annual certification budgets, technical training, and paid conference access.

Absolute Ownership

We work in small, focused units. Every engineer is trusted to design, validate, and support their network segments with autonomy.

Privacy Obsession

We treat client data payloads as sacred. We maintain a strict no-logs transit standard, building secure transit pipelines rather than data monitors.

Collaborative Spirit

Even as a remote-first team, we sync daily, run collaborative code audits, and stand up solutions together in peer programming sessions.
