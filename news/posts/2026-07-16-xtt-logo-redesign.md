Today, we are thrilled to unveil XTT's new dynamic brand identity. Our redesigned logo represents our focus on secure, identity-aware networking and managed carrier services.

### A Focus on Visual Clarity and Contrast

Our new logo design features bold, heavy typography matched with high-contrast geometric lines. To align with our client portal's default light theme, we have established dual visual paths:
- **Light Theme**: A crisp, stark black logo designed to stand out on clean, glassmorphic headers.
- **Dark Theme**: An inverted, bright white logo that adapts dynamically as users switch their system preferences.

This visual system extends to our partners' assets, including Fortinet, MikroTik, HPE, Tenable, and InHand Networks. By utilizing clean vector rendering, we ensure that every logo element scales perfectly on high-resolution displays. We are proud to roll out this update across all client dashboards, marketing collateral, and billing templates starting this week.
