About XTT Ltd. Co.

Designing secure network pathways that connect enterprises to global workloads.

Our Mission & Background

Established in Atlanta, Georgia, XTT Ltd. Co. was founded to resolve the challenges of rigid legacy network design, cloud migrations, and remote office connectivity. We build and operate high-availability, software-defined network overlays that bypass local carrier restrictions and dynamically secure traffic boundaries directly at the edge.

Unlike traditional telecoms that rely on single backbones and vendor lock-in, XTT is carrier-neutral. We consolidate local ISPs, manage BGP routing across regional core PoPs (Atlanta, Dallas, Seattle), and optimize path stability to ensure your enterprise workloads perform seamlessly.

Core Corporate Pillars

Carrier Independence

We are dedicated to vendor neutrality. We bond whatever first-mile paths are best at your locations (fiber, 5G, satellite Starlink) to build absolute uptime redundancy.

Data Sovereignty

We respect client privacy. XTT operates under a strict no-logs network transit standard for all data payloads—we secure your routes, we never inspect your files.

Uptime Transparency

We believe in verifiable performance. Our network SLA targets (98% Core availability) are backed by clear, contractually guaranteed service credits.

Integration Competence

We connect and optimize. Our professional Edge AI consulting designs low-latency transport overlays configured safely for private local GPU clusters.
